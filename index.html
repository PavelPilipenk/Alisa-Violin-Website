About

Alisa has been teaching violin and piano for over 15 years. Her lesson plans are inspired by the student, her own education, and her 18 years performing as a professional musician. Primarily a classical violinist with a Bachelor's degree in violin performance, Alisa has been improvising and composing in bands her whole life.

"I like to find what the student excels at and encourage them to go in that direction. The thing about technique is as you gain skill music becomes more fun, so the student naturally DOES want to learn if the teacher can guide them in the right direction. With each little win, they are inspired to get another little win... next thing you know we are making music simply - like it's a language." - Alisa C.

Lessons

Violin

Violin lessons with Alisa are tailored to the students' interests and goals in music. Alisa bases her lessons classically to develop a strong technical foundation while approaching her lesson plan with the student’s life and musical interests in mind. Ready to achieve fluency on the violin? Contact us to book violin lessons.

"I prefer to teach the Sassmannshaus method, which is popular in Germany for the young violinists. As they grow in their technique, we will begin to introduce classic etudes and orchestral repertoire that can win a student an audition or provide technical fluency to be able to just PLAY." - Alisa

Piano

Piano is the best introductory instrument in music and beautifully translates into any other instrument the student grows into. Interested in starting to learn the musical "language"? Contact us to book piano lessons.
